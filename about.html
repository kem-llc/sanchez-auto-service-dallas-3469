Our story

Family-Owned Auto Repair Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair that keeps Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

Sanchez Auto Service was founded in 1995 by Master Technician Carlos Sanchez with a simple philosophy: treat every customer like family. Starting from a small two-bay shop, Carlos built a reputation for honest diagnostics and fair pricing. Over the years, the business grew as word spread about the team's dedication to quality.

Today, Sanchez Auto Service continues that tradition with a team of experienced mechanics who share the same values. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to serve the evolving needs of modern vehicles. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we approach every job with the same commitment to excellence.
