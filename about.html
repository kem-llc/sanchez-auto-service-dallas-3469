Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Auto Repair Shop Since 1985

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair and maintenance that keeps our community safe on the road.

Sanchez Auto Service was founded in 1985 by Carlos Sanchez, a master mechanic with a passion for cars and a commitment to integrity. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into a full-service auto repair center trusted by thousands of Dallas drivers. Carlos's philosophy was simple: treat every customer like family and every car like it's your own.

Today, his son Miguel carries on that tradition. Our team of ASE-certified technicians continues to deliver the same honest service and expert repairs that have been our hallmark for over three decades. We invest in the latest diagnostic technology and ongoing training to ensure we can handle any vehicle—from a 1990s sedan to the latest luxury SUV.
