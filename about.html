Our story

Your Trusted Auto Repair Shop in Dallas, TX

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and mobile.

Sanchez Auto Service has been serving the Dallas community for over 30 years. Founded by master mechanic Juan Sanchez, our shop started as a small garage with a big commitment to quality. Over the decades, we've grown by earning the trust of thousands of customers through reliable repairs and transparent service.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of certified technicians who share a passion for cars and customer care. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine overhaul, we treat every vehicle as if it were our own. We believe in educating our customers so they can make informed decisions about their vehicles.
